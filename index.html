Our Story

Bringing Tanzania's Wonders to Every Child

Founded in 2015, Kenneth Children Foundation was born from a simple truth: Tanzania's children are surrounded by some of the world's greatest natural and cultural treasures — yet many have never experienced them.

🦒

Wildlife Discovery Kits

Hands-on educational materials featuring Tanzania's iconic animals, helping children learn through play and storytelling.

🗺️

Heritage Map Programs

Interactive maps and field trips connecting children to national parks, historical sites, and coastal wonders of Tanzania.

🎨

Playful Curriculum Design

We co-create tourism literacy materials with educators — puzzles, games, books, and digital tools — to make learning joyful.

What We Do

Our Programs

From classrooms to conservation areas, our programs spark curiosity and build pride in Tanzania's extraordinary heritage.

🦁

Junior Safari Explorer

Guided wildlife discovery days in national parks, paired with illustrated animal journals for children aged 7–14. Safety-first, fun-always.

📚

Tourism Literacy in Schools

We deliver engaging tourism education directly into classrooms — covering ecology, culture, history and sustainable travel practices.

Support this →
🎭

Cultural Play Workshops

Interactive storytelling, Swahili songs, tribal art, and traditional dance — children explore their own culture as tourism ambassadors.

Support this →
🏔️

Kilimanjaro Dream Camp

Annual mountain ecology camp where selected students spend 3 days at the foothills, learning conservation and environmental stewardship.

Support this →
🐠

Coastal Discovery Tours

Zanzibar and Dar es Salaam shoreline exploration — teaching marine ecosystems, history of the Swahili coast, and ocean conservation.

Support this →
🎮

Digital Tourism Games

Tablet-based interactive games that simulate safari decision-making, ecosystem balance, and responsible tourism choices for children.
